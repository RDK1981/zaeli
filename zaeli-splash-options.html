My take

Option B (Warm Cream + Coral) is my pick. It matches the in-app warm white background perfectly, so the transition from splash → Chat is seamless (no colour flash, no weird tonal shift). The coral "a" and "i" tie directly to the Send button and app's primary action colour. The soft orbs add depth without feeling heavy. The pulse dot signals "I'm ready."

Option A (Full Lavender) is strongest for brand boldness — the moment the app opens, lavender = Zaeli. Chat is home, lavender is Chat's colour. But the transition to the in-app warm white feels abrupt.

Option C (Deep Slate + Mint) is the most cinematic. Feels like a meditation app. Premium, calming. But may feel at odds with Zaeli's daytime warm energy — the in-app is bright, this splash is dark.

Quick verdict: B if you want continuity and warmth, A if you want bold brand identity, C if you want cinematic premium. Pick one (or a hybrid) and I'll build it.
