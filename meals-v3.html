Ingredients · Serves 4
🍝Penne pasta500gIn pantry
🍗Chicken thighs500gIn pantry
🥫Tomato sauce2 cansIn pantry
🧀Mozzarella200gIn pantry
🧅Onion1 largeIn pantry
🧄Garlic3 clovesIn pantry
Method

1. Preheat oven to 200°C. Cook pasta until al dente, drain and set aside.

2. Fry onion and garlic until soft, add chicken and cook through. Add tomato sauce and simmer 5 mins.

3. Combine pasta and sauce in a baking dish. Top with mozzarella and bake 20 mins until golden.
