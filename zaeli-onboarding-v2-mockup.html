Changes from v1

  • Screen count 12 → 13 — added a dedicated "show me a photo, ask me anything" screen (Screen 10). This is the sticky-feature screen and it earns its slot.
  • ElevenLabs voice pill on Screen 2 — tap to hear Zaeli's voice. First-impression wow, lands before any friction.
  • Email collected in Screen 3 (alongside name) — one card, two fields, single submit. No second ask.
  • Kids' year levels shown on Screen 4 — needed for Tutor to pitch level-appropriate help.
  • Preferences bundled into Screen 6 — holiday plans, shared shopping, monthly budget added as toggles under the stress chips. All under one "What matters right now" card.
  • Permissions screen (07) — location + notifications, framed around user benefit, not product need. "3 a day, max" cap promise.
  • Family colours locked — Poppy purple, Gab green, Duke amber, Rich blue, Anna coral (matches Kids Hub / Our Family / Travel palette).
  • Brief card pixel-matched — Screen 11 brief is the real brief (peach bubble, black star on sky eyebrow, 17px body, mint win banner, coral-tinted primary chip).
  • Copy rewritten for wit — Zaeli has more personality throughout. "So no one's buying three jars of mustard" / "You get your dinner hour back" / "I'll keep an eye on Duke". "You've got this" and "chaos" earn their places.
  • "Set this up later" skip link on every non-critical card (rhythm, chips, permissions) — lowers pressure and abandon rate.

Design-system sync — mechanical

  • Wordmark: z<a-sky>el<i-sky> — each letter separately tinted (not ae together)
  • Chat bar: unified pill — mic · sep · input · camera · send

  • Body bg: #FAF8F5 warm white
  • Brief bubble: #FAC8A8 peach · 17px Poppins body · mint win banner
