Hard rules

Design rules

Things that don't change. Ever. These are the decisions that make Zaeli feel like one coherent product.

🎨
Body is always warm white. #FAF8F5 on every screen, every state. Not pure white, not off-white, not system default.
🔴
Send button is always coral. #FF4545 everywhere — chat send, form submit, primary CTA. No exceptions.
🔤
Poppins for all UI text. DM Serif Display is reserved exclusively for ghost/decorative numbers. Never for readable content.
📱
No tab bar, no hamburger, no nav chrome. ZaeliFAB is the only navigation element. One surface, always the same position.
📋
Channels open as 92% sheets. Calendar, Shopping, Meals, Todos, Notes, Travel — never dedicated screens, never router.navigate(). Tutor, Kids Hub, Our Family, Settings are the only full screens.
🗑
Delete is always optimistic. Remove from UI immediately. Supabase happens in background. Two-tap confirm before destructive action.
📐
Cards follow the headline formula. One bold Poppins statement. 24px on full-width cards, 26–28px on the half-width Zaeli Noticed card. Data lives behind the tap.
⬜
Every expanded card has a full-width button. borderRadius:14, paddingVertical:14, Poppins_700Bold 15px. Consistent across Calendar, Dinner, Shopping, Actions.
🌏
Local dates always. Never toISOString(), never +10:00 suffix. Always construct local date strings directly to avoid UTC/AEST day-shift bugs.
✦
The ✦ mark means My Space only. Never used decoratively. It is the exclusive navigation symbol for the My Space screen — its meaning must stay singular.
